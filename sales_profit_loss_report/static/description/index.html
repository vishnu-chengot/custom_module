Sales Profit and Loss Report

This module helps to analyze the Profit and Loss from Sale Orders in your company. It will helps to download report in pdf and excel both for Profit and Loss of your Confirmed Sale Orders. You can change selling price of the product day by day and it will show a new line for every unit cost change of particular product. In report you can find the Amount W/O tax(Price Subtotal * Quantity) and Cost Price(Total Cost * Quantity) for particular product between selected dates.

Features

  • Helps to get report of your sales profit or loss.
  • Easy to use and flexible with selected dates.
  • Export PDF and Excel report easily.
  • Selected Users can access the report menu in Sale.
  • Different Selling Price(Unit Price) of same product cause the new line in report for accurate result in report.
